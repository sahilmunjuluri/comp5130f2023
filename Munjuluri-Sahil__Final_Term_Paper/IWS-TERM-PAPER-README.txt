


Abstract:
This study meticulously compares fuel prices across gas stations, focusing on the influence of geographical proximity. Addressing global concerns about energy sustainability, it explores factors contributing to variations in fuel costs. Data from diverse stations is analysed using a geospatial approach and GIS tools. The results offer. 
valuable insights into fuel pricing dynamics, empowering consumers, guiding policymakers, and aiding businesses in optimizing strategies. This research contributes nuanced perspectives to the evolving discourse on fuel pricing, fostering transparency, economic efficiency, and informed decision-making in the fuel sector.
